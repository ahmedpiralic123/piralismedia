FAQ

Pricing questions?

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is there a free trial or consultation?+

Yes — we offer a free 30-minute discovery call for all new clients to discuss your goals before any commitment.

Can I change plans later?+

Absolutely. You can upgrade or change your plan at any time. We'll pro-rate any difference and make the transition seamless.

Are there any setup fees?+

No setup fees on any plan. The price you see is what you pay — nothing hidden.

Do you offer one-time project pricing?+

Yes. If you need a one-off website build or a specific project rather than ongoing retainer work, we can quote a fixed project price. Get in touch to discuss.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept bank transfers, major credit cards, and PayPal. Invoicing is monthly or annually depending on your chosen billing cycle.
